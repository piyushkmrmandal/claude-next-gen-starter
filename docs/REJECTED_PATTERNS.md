# REJECTED PATTERNS

These patterns are explicitly forbidden in this project.

## Layout Patterns
- ❌ 3-column "feature card" grids
- ❌ Centered hero with illustration to the right
- ❌ Cookie-cutter pricing tables (3 tiers, centered, equal width)
- ❌ Full-page background gradient
- ❌ Floating startup-style emoji/icon decorations

## Visual Patterns
- ❌ Excessive glassmorphism on every surface
- ❌ Neon glow effects without purpose
- ❌ Multiple drop shadows stacked
- ❌ Gradient text on gradient background
- ❌ Stock photo hero backgrounds

## Motion Patterns
- ❌ Bouncy spring on navigation transitions
- ❌ Elements floating or pulsing on idle
- ❌ Rotation animations on hover
- ❌ Scroll-triggered confetti or particle bursts
- ❌ Page transitions that take over 800ms

## Typography Patterns
- ❌ "Gradient text" as a primary headline technique
- ❌ Mixed weights within a single heading
- ❌ Ultra-condensed fonts for body text
- ❌ All-caps headings longer than 5 words

## Component Patterns
- ❌ Generic SaaS testimonial carousels
- ❌ Auto-playing video backgrounds
- ❌ Cookie consent banners that block content
- ❌ Chat widget overlays on portfolio sites
