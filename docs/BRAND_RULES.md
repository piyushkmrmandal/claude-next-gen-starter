# BRAND RULES

## Visual Identity
- No stock photography
- No generic icons from icon packs without customization
- Illustration style must be consistent and purposeful
- Color palette: 2 primary, 1 accent, neutral scale

## Tone
- Direct and confident, not salesy
- Technical precision without jargon
- Human warmth without being casual

## Do Not
- Use competitor brand colors
- Mix more than 2 typeface families
- Apply drop shadows to text
- Use colored text on colored backgrounds without 4.5:1 contrast
